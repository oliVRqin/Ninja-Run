Final Project Attributes 

The player and enemy assets came from the Pixel Adventure 1 assets on the Unity Store.The creator's name is Pixel Frog. Assets used are in the Mask Dude and Ninja Frog folders. Link to the official website: https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/2d/characters/pixel-adventure-1-155360. 

The sound came from the Fantasy Arcade RPG Freedom World Sounds assets on the Unity Store, here:
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/audio/music/fantasy-arcade-rpg-freedom-world-sounds-free-package-123530#description. Creator name is Poligons Sounds. Assets are in the MP3 folder.
